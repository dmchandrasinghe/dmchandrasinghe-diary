# ⚡ EV Vehicle

Electric vehicle technology, charging infrastructure, protocols, and automotive software engineering notes.

## Sub-topics

- **Charging Infrastructure** – EVSE design, hardware integration, site planning
- **OCPP Protocol** – Open Charge Point Protocol 1.6 / 2.0.1 — messages, flows, implementation
- **Battery Management** – BMS architecture, State of Charge, thermal management
- **Fleet Management** – EV fleet platforms, telematics, scheduling, optimisation
- **Automotive Software** – AUTOSAR, OTA updates, V2X, embedded systems
- **Standards & Compliance** – ISO 15118, IEC 61851, OCPI, OSCP
- **Smart Grid Integration** – V2G, demand response, energy management systems
