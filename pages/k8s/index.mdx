# ☸ Kubernetes (k8s)

Notes, guides, and learnings around Kubernetes — container orchestration, cluster management, and cloud-native patterns.

## Sub-topics

- **Cluster Setup** – Bootstrapping clusters with kubeadm, kind, k3s, AKS, EKS, GKE
- **Workloads** – Deployments, StatefulSets, DaemonSets, Jobs, CronJobs
- **Networking** – Services, Ingress, Network Policies, CNI plugins
- **Storage** – Persistent Volumes, StorageClasses, CSI drivers
- **Security** – RBAC, Pod Security, OPA/Gatekeeper, Secrets management
- **Operators & CRDs** – Writing operators, using Operator Framework
- **Monitoring & Observability** – Prometheus, Grafana, Loki, OpenTelemetry
- **Helm** – Chart authoring, releases, repositories
- **GitOps with ArgoCD / Flux** – GitOps workflows and continuous delivery
